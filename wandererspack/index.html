Welcome to the wanderer's pack.

"Anticipate Happiness"

The Wanderer’s Pack is a short-term time capsule dedicated to the exploration of your dream destination. The interactive backpack you can fill with your location, story of why, and with who you want to bring along. Once completed and opened on May 14th you will be able to reflect on your destination through an interactive map.

The goal of The Wanderer’s Pack project is to create a discussion and provoke one’s thoughts on dream destinations and how one’s ideas or thoughts of these trips can change in a relatively short amount of time.
